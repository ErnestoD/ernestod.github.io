Greenville, SC

The Bon Secours Wellness Arena is a state-of-the-art 15,000 seat sports and entertainment venue perfectly situated between Greenville's award-winning Main Street and Interstate 385, a major thoroughfare. The entertainment industry has consistently recognized the Bon Secours Wellness Arena as one of the top fifty venues in the world.More than 400,000 people walk through the doors of the Arena annually, leading to an over $60 million impact on the Greenville metro area.
