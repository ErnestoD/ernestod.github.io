Bio

Born and raised in Japan with his family, which consists of his father, Soichiro Yagami, who is a member of the Japanese Task Force (an investigation team opposing the terrorist Kira), his mother, Sachiko Yagami, and his younger sister, Sayu Yagami.

Mr. Yagami has been noted as hardworking, talented, and a natural genius. He would constantly predict all of the possible scenarios that could happen and plan a solution in advance, even in the most impromptu situations.

Being frustrated by the lack of justice in the world, his main goal is to create a new world that is free of injustice and populated only with honest and kind people.
